Red Sauce Pasta

Serving Size

400 g

About

Delicious red sauce pasta, slow cooked in tangy tomato sauce. Served in a simple, authentic Italian way. This is a great meal for brunch or to pack in your kids tiffin. Enjoyed immensely by adults and kids alike.

Ingredients

  • 5-6 Tomatoes (diced)
  • 1 Galic clove
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 Cup of Water
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 4-5 Basil Leaves
  • 1 tbsp Oninos, chopped
  • 1/2 tbsp Garlic, chopped
