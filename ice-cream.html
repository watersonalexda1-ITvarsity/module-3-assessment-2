Thandai Ice Cream

Serving Size

120 g

About

A smooth and creamy ice cream , with the flavours of almonds, rose water melon seeds and cardamom. A heavenly dessert to serve at the next dinner party!

Ingredients

  • 250 MI Milk
  • 500 MI Cream
  • 1/2 Cup Castor Sugar
  • 4 Egg Yolks
  • 3 Cardamom Seeds
  • 1 tsp Black Peppercorns
  • 1 tsp Coriander Seeds
  • 1 tsp Melon Seeds
  • 1/2 Cup Almonds(blanched)
  • 1 tsp Ginger Powder
  • 1 tbsp Cashewnut
  • 1/4 tsp Nutmeg Powder
  • 1 tsp Pistachio
  • 1/2 Saffron
  • 1 tsp Saunf
