Chocolate Cake

Serving Size

200 g

About

A gooey, mushy serving of chocolate is all that is needed to lift up your spirits,it is a bonus when you can cook it in a jiffy! Create this sensational chocolate cake with ease and delight

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cup Flour (sifted)
  • 1 Cup Cocoa Powder (unsweetened)
  • 2 Cup Sugar(sifted)
  • 1 Cup Oil
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Coffee
