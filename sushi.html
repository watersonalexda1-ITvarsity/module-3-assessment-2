Exotic Lovers Roll

Serving Size

400 g

About

Cucumber, avocado along with crab sticks filled into sushi ricr rolls, sums up the delicious appetizer that you won't be able to resist. The perfect snack fot dinner night with your loved ones.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Lemon Juice
  • 1 Medium Avocado(pitted and sliced into 1/4 inch thick pieces), peeled
  • 4 Sheets Nori
  • 1/3 Cup Sesame seeds (toasted)
  • 1 Small Cucumber(seeded, and cut into matchsticks-size pieces), peeled
  • 4 Crabsticks (cut into pieces)
  • 1/4 Cup Salmon Roe (Egg)
  • Ginger (pickled)
  • Wasabi
  • Soy Sauce
